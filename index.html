FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting funded through KLIERD.

What credit score do I need to qualify? +
We work with clients starting at 620. Clients below 620 may qualify for our Credit Profile Optimization services first, which typically improves scores within 30–45 days before funding.
Do you work with ITIN clients? +
Yes. We proudly serve ITIN clients with full bilingual support in English and Spanish. You do not need a Social Security Number to get started. Our team understands your unique situation and will guide you through every step.
How much funding can I get? +
Personal funding ranges from $5,000 to $100,000+. Business funding ranges from $25,000 to $575,000 depending on your credit profile, time in business, and revenue. Startup capital ranges from $25,000 to $125,000.
How long does the process take? +
Most clients are funded within 30–45 days. Clients who need credit profile optimization first typically see results within 30 days before moving to the funding phase.
Is this a credit repair service? +
No. KLIERD is an Intelligent Funding Technology platform. We offer integrated dispute resolution services and credit profile optimization as part of our funding preparation process. Results are not typical and may vary.
What is the success fee? +
Our success fee is 15% of the funded amount, charged only after you are funded. There is also a $500 post-funding technology fee. Service fees for credit profile optimization are separate and discussed during your consultation.
