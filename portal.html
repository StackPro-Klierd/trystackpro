What Is a Shelf Corporation?
A shelf corporation (or aged shelf company) is a legally registered business entity that was created years ago and has been kept "on the shelf" — never used, no liabilities, no tax filings required. Clients purchase them to instantly gain an aged business entity for credit applications, banking, and contracts.
Why Clients Want Them
Lenders require 2+ years in business for most loans. An aged shelf corp instantly satisfies that requirement — no waiting period.
⚠️ Critical Warning
Some lenders run SOS verification and date-check the entity. Never misrepresent business activity on applications. The shelf corp gives age — not fabricated revenue history.
